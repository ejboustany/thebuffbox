FAQ's

1. How does Buffbox work?

You’ll take a short questionnaire, and we’ll recommend products tailored to your goals. Then you can subscribe to receive a box of 4 samples and get access to exclusive member benefits.

2. What’s in each box?

Each box contains 4 single-serving samples—2 you select and 2 “power picks” we choose to help you explore new products.

3. Can I return a box?

Boxes are non-returnable. However, you can update your preferences anytime so we can tailor your future boxes more accurately.

4. How do I manage or cancel my subscription?

Log in to your account to pick your delivery schedule (every 30, 45, or 60 days), make changes, or cancel at any time.
